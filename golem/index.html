materials

This dome was made of only 1/2" electrical conduit (EMT), 2 1/2"-1/4" bolts, washers, and standard nuts. The main challenge in material selection was trying to optimize conduit usage since it only comes in 10' rods. After deciding on the dimensions of my design/each strut of conduit, I filled a sheet with every potential combination of strut lengths that would fit within the 10' strut length available for purchase. I found that a few options for cutting the struts would give me the same amount of waste... -------- The EMT was best-priced at The Home Depot.
